Sam Harris, in a podcast with Susan Cain, discussed some of the characteristics of AI. He talked about how some people will and do use AI to mentor them, and that the AI will seem better than any human mentor. The antisocial effects of this can be damaging. I see myself in this picture with my Sunrise Briefing and how I use it to work on my insight practice. It offers help that I can't get locally, conveniently, or at a reasonable price.
